IMPORTANT NOTES:
  - The init job runs DB migrations and registers chain/contracts automatically.
  - TXS workers include the "processing" queue (required for TXS v5.42.1).
  - CGW has its own dedicated PostgreSQL database (required since v1.80+).
  - Safe UI requires a custom Docker build with NEXT_PUBLIC_* vars baked in at
    build time (Next.js DefinePlugin) and safe-deployments patched for chain {{ .Values.l1.evmChainId }}.
  - Post-build sed replacement of hardcoded safe-client.safe.global URLs is also
    required for the UI to use your gateway instead of the public Safe gateway.
